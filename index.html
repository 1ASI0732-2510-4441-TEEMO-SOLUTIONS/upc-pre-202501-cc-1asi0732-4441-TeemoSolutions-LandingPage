Mushroom

Who we are?

Mushroom is a university startup dedicated to optimizing maritime logistics through intelligent route planning. Born from academic research and shaped by real-world challenges, we empower exporters, importers, and shipping operators to make faster, smarter decisions in a volatile global trade environment.

What is our mission?

Our mission is to make global maritime logistics safer, more predictable, and more efficient by delivering real-time, intelligent routing solutions that adapt to geopolitical, climatic, and operational disruptions.

Our Services

At Mushroom, we help maritime logistics professionals stay ahead. Our platform includes:

Real-Time Route Optimization.

Real-time route optimization using live maritime, weather, and geopolitical data for dynamic decision-making.

Smart Alerts for Critical Event.

Receive smart alerts on storms, port congestion, and conflict zones to stay ahead of disruptions.

Alternative Route Simulation

Simulate alternative routes with risk assessments to choose the safest and most efficient path.

Predictive Disruption Analytics

Predict disruptions before they happen using smart forecasting powered by historical and live data.

Delay and Cost Estimation

Estimate delays and costs per route to optimize planning and control shipping expenses.

Integrated with Your Logistics Ecosystem

Easily sync with your ERP, TMS, and other core systems.
