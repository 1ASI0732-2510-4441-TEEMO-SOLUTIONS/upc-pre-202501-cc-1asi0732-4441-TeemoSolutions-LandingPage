About Us the GPT4 team presents:
MOVEO

Who we are?

MOVEO is a university startup dedicated to connecting workers who need reliable transportation with people willing to lend their vehicles safely and affordably.

What is our mission?

Our mission is to democratize access to vehicles through a fair and flexible lending model, promoting mobility as a right and an opportunity for thousands of workers.

Subscriptions

At Moveo, we work with a vehicle subscription model, where users pay for the time they use the car they choose, in a flexible and affordable way.

What we offer

Our Services

At Moveo, we connect people who need to move with available cars near them, offering a flexible, safe, and collaborative way to rent and generate income.

📅 Custom Time Rental

Choose how long you need the car: hours, days, or weeks. You only pay for the time you use it.

🔍 Nearby Car Search

Find available vehicles in parking lots near your location, ready to be used.

📍 Nearby Drop-Off and Pick-Up

Cars are left in designated parking lots near you for added convenience.

💸 Passive Income Generation

Suppliers can put their cars on the road during their chosen times and earn hassle-free.

📲 App-Based Management

Monitor vehicle status, loan duration, payments, and more, all from a single platform.

🕒 Availability and Recall Control

Car owners decide when to borrow and when to retrieve their car. Complete control at all times.
